## Thorium Reader iOS

Thorium Reader for iOS brings accessible, feature-rich EPUB reading to your iPhone and iPad. As a native iOS application, it provides seamless integration with Apple's ecosystem while maintaining the same commitment to accessibility and customization that defines Thorium Reader. Currently in beta, Thorium iOS is actively being refined with community feedback.

### Platform Information

- **Operating System**: iOS and iPadOS (Apple devices)
- **Status**: Beta (TestFlight)
- **Focus**: Native iOS experience with full accessibility support
- **License**: Open Source
- **Community**: Active beta testing and feedback program
